Overview

  • Wentao Wang (Rick) is a undergraduate researcher at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research focuses on developing novel methodologies to improve the Medical Image Analysis and Surgical Robot Design. At present, he is working on medical image segmentation at The State Key Laboratory for Management and Control of Complex Systems, Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and he will join the Visual Analytics and Imaging (VAI) Lab in August 2023 to work on medical image generation. Wentao has been invited to be the reviewer of IEEE ICARM 2023 and CAA SAFEPROCESS 2023. And Wentao's github gets 70+ stars.
